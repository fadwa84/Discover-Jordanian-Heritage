Jordanian Heritage Patterns – Symbols Guide

Below are the four patterns used in the app, with their symbolic meanings.
Bedouin Rug
Symbolizes protection, home, and hospitality. Woven by Bedouins for centuries.
Jordanian Shmagh
Represents identity, pride, and unity. A signature pattern of Jordan.
Madaba Mosaic
Reflects the rich mosaic heritage of Madaba, famed for its artistry.
Straw Basket
Stands for craftsmanship and harmony with nature through traditional weaving.
